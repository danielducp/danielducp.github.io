About New Theatre Peterborough

We’re looking forward to welcoming you to the New Theatre, Peterborough
With two fully-stocked bars to choose from, you are welcome to enjoy a relaxing pre-show drink from one hour before the performance. Our spacious Circle Bar offers plenty of seating and stunning floor-to-ceiling windows and the recent addition of our beautiful baby grand piano. We are proud to serve Café Praego coffee, ensuring that we ‘give a little back’ with every cup sold, alongside speciality teas, and comforting hot chocolates. Look out for our Tea & Cake offer on certain shows and treat yourself to something special before the show.
