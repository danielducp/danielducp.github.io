About Ferry Meadows

The ideal place for family day out. One of the largest country parks in the region. Situated at the heart of Nene Park, its varied landscapes make it the favourite destination for thousands of visitors throughout the year. Whether you’re looking to walk, cycle, ride a train, sail a boat, enjoy a cup of tea in the café or go fishing. Ferry Meadows is the place to explore endless meadows, lakes and woodland – all less than three miles from Peterborough city centre. We run a packed events programme and education programme throughout the year, offering the chance to learn new skills and to discover more about this incredible Park. As well as offering a great day out for friends, families and schools Ferry Meadows is also the perfect place to organise an event, take part in a team day with colleagues or volunteer. Easy to get to and open 365 days a year, make Ferry Meadows your next great day out.
